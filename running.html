Meal one: Multi coloured pasta with feta and chicken

  • Start by boiling the pasta
  • Now crumble up feta
  • Add spinaxch to a pan and wilt it for 5 minutes
  • Once the pasta is cooked, remove it from the heat and add tghe spinach in along with salt, pepper and lemon juice
  • Lastly, cook some chicken on a pan with cream sauce and olive oil
  • Add the feta and chicken into the bowl of pasta, stir and enjoy!

Meal 2: Chicken and aubergine bake with brown rice

  • Cut up your aubergine and chicken
  • Add a tomasto based sauce to heat and add in herbs as well as salt and pepper and a squeeze of lemon
  • Cut up garlic and onion and add it to the chicken and aubergine in a baking dish
  • Sprinkle breadcrumbs over the chicken and pour over the tomatoe sauce
  • Bake at 180 degrees fopr an hour
  • While this is ahppening cook your brown rice
  • Now serve up and enjoy!
