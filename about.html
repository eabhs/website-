Tip Number 1

Speed and endurance

It is important when you are running to pace yourself and go at a steasdy pace that you can maintain throughout the run, if it is long distance running you are doing, or a marathon. It is also good to know what points in the run you should speed up at a little bit, and which parts you should slow down. It is good to slow down on an uphill, and run fast ebfore that top build up to it. Yoiu can speed up on an uphill, but make sure that the pace you are goling at is not one you will burn out with. You also want to increase your speed as you go on, not the other way round. /p>

Tip number 2

What to do to prepare

To prepare for your run, you should make sure you eat a filling, healthy meal one to two hours before your run, as well as do some stretches. For example, you should stretch in ways that involves movement, for example leg swings that activate the muscle. Make sure you are hydrated, properly dressed and fueled.
